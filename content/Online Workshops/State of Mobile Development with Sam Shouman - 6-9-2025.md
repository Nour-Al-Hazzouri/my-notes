# 6/9/2025 - State of Mobile Development with Sam Shouman
## Meeting Notes
- **Mobile dev has higher salaries than web development in general due to the large amount of people who choose web development over mobile dev**
- **The shift from web dev to mobile dev is easier than expected, some people from web dev might even have better logic than some mobile devs due to working with javascript, and the only defferences are programming languages and development environments**
- **Web dev is broad with many option for both frontend and backend, while mobile dev is mostly kotlin for android and swift for ios. Usually android studio is used for android and xcode for ios**
- **There's no definite answer to 'What is harder/better, mobile or web dev?', it's pretty much what you like and what you don't. Choose what fits you**
- **Using AI is becoming mandatory in enterprises, like Sam Shimoun's company where it's now necessary to use Ai to boost producivity, he even mentioned an example, that he heard a developer got fired from a finance company due to his inexperience to use AI**

### Q&A
#### Q: If a web developer who's skilled at his job, uses a framework like react native to create mobile apps, what are mobile dev companies's prespective to him? Can he be hired as a mobile dev even though he's natively a web dev?
#### Y: Yes he can, when hiring, his mindset or skills might impress the HR more than native mobile devs. It mostly depends on the required skillset and what the company wants.
